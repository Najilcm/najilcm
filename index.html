Interactive Sales Dashboard

A comprehensive dashboard for real-time sales tracking and analysis.

Project Overview

Developed a dynamic and interactive sales dashboard in Power BI to provide stakeholders with a clear view of key performance indicators (KPIs), sales trends over time, and regional performance breakdowns. The goal was to replace static, weekly reports with a live, on-demand analytics tool.

The dashboard integrated data from multiple SQL databases, which was cleaned and transformed to ensure accuracy. It features several interactive elements like slicers for date ranges, product categories, and regions, allowing for deep-dive analysis. The insights from this dashboard helped the sales team identify top-performing products and underperforming regions, leading to a more targeted sales strategy.
